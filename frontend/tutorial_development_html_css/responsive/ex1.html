About us

I am not a social butterfly kind of person due to which I don’t really like hanging out with other people except for a few close ones. Therefore, I try to explore some quiet, less-crowded, and unconventional coffee shops near my locality.
is one little, aesthetic cafe a few hundred meters away from my house which I frequently visit. Although I go to that coffee every weekend, if I get time I also go there even on some weekdays. Unlike the other popular and crowded coffee shops in my vicinity, the ambiance here is very serene and refreshing which enchants me every time I visit it.
Moreover, the café owner, fortunately, plays classical music in a mild volume which complements the overall coffee-sipping, mind-refreshing experience. Also, the freshly brewed filtered coffee served by them along with sweet pancakes feels like heaven in the mouth.
